Our Story

Built on Trust & Hard Work



Moon Light Car Care was founded with one simple goal — to provide honest, fast and affordable car care to the people of Abu Dhabi. What started as a small battery shop in Al Danah has grown into one of Abu Dhabi's most trusted auto care destinations.

For over 8 years, we have served hundreds of customers across Abu Dhabi — from everyday drivers to fleet owners. Whether it's a dead battery in the middle of the road or an AC that stops cooling in the UAE summer heat, we are the team people call.

We believe in no hidden charges, no unnecessary upselling — just honest advice and quality workmanship. That's why our customers keep coming back and recommending us to their friends and family.

Located at Al Funoud St, Al Danah – Zone 1, Abu Dhabi, we are open every day from 8:00 AM to 11:30 PM — including weekends and public holidays.
